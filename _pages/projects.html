Research & ML

A Holographic Brain for LLMs

A fixed-size associative memory for language models, based on Holographic Reduced Representations. Hit 100% recall at 2,600 facts with a ~42× compression ratio versus a naive vector database—after debugging a catastrophic cross-talk failure down to embedding orthogonality.

HRR · NumPy · FAISS · SentenceTransformers

A Sandbox for Human Behavior

300 AI personas with distinct personalities and ideologies, exposed to 3,000 real tweets, producing 3,317 engagement events with realistic power-law dynamics and traceable decision paths—a research instrument for asking why people engage (or stay silent).
